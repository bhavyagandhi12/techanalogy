Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in this robotics workshop?

Our robotics workshop is open to anyone with a passion for robotics and a desire to learn. Participants can be students, working professionals, or individuals interested in the field of robotics.

What materials and equipment are provided?

We take pride in providing comprehensive support to our participants. Rest assured, we will ensure that all the necessary materials and equipment are readily available for your workshop experience. This includes top-notch robot kits, essential electronic components, a wide array of tools, and the required software.

What is the duration of the workshop?

The duration of the workshop may vary depending on the specific programme. Every programme will have its doubt-clearing sessions along with live sessions to clear each student’s problems and difficulties.

Are there any age restrictions for participation?

 There is no age restriction for participation in the workshop. Our workshops are designed to cater to a wide age range. While some programmes may have specific age requirements, we generally welcome participants of all ages. However, younger participants may require parental consent
